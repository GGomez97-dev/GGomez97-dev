    <h1>Avocado Toast</h1>
    <p>
      Recipe to make a simple avoado toast
    </p>
    <h2>
      Ingredients
    </h2>
      <ul>
        <li>Slice of bread</li>
        <li>Avocado</li>
        <li>Olive Oil</li>
        <li>Salt and Pepper</li>
        <li>Garlic Powder</li>
        <li>Lemon</li>
        <li> Chili Flakes / Cayene Pepper</li>
      </ul>
    <h2>
      Instructions
    </h2>
      <ol>
        <li>Toast bread</li>
        <li>Skin avocado and mash in bowl.</li>
        <li>Add in salt, pepper, garlic powder, and a squeeze of lemon.</li>
        <li>Take avocado mix and spread across toast.</li>
        <li>After spread is done, sprinkle your choice of chili flakes or cayene pepper to the top of the avocado.</li>
        <li>Finally, drizzle olive oil acros the top of the avocado toast and enjoy!</li>
      </ol>
